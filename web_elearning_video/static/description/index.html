Record and Embed Audio or Video in Html Editor

Enhance Odoo Html editor(Summernote)

Key Highlights

  • Enable html editor of odoo by enabling recording feature.
  • Support audio recording and embed.
  • Support video recording and embed.
  • Support download after record.
  • Recording icons come by default in odoo studio html editors.
  • Except Odoo Studio, developer need to add audio, video tag in html toolbar. Screen is attached at bottom.

Screenshots

After install, audio/video icons is enabled inside html editor to record audio/video respectivily.

Once you click on video record icon, it will ask camera permission to access video. You can start recording then

Play or Download video after recording, just to preview before embed.

Click on save to embed video in editor.

Once you click on Record audio icon, it will ask microphone permission system microphone to record.

Play or Download audio after recording.

Click on save to embed audio in editor.

To add these plugins in website and other modules. Add ['video', ['video', 'audio']] in wysiwyg editor toolbar.
